What Makes Us Different

Not your average parenting app

🔄

Mom Recharge Hours

Take turns hosting each other's kids. You watch theirs — they watch yours. Two actual free hours every week.

🎖️

Playdate Passport

Kids collect stamps at each park and venue in your city. Parents level up too.

🌧️

Monsoon Mud Days

Let kids get delightfully dirty in the rain. Parents bond over chai. Because perfectly clean kids are overrated.

❤️

The Honest Hour

Weekly anonymous check-in: "How are you REALLY doing?" — No toxic positivity, just real talk. 😴🆘😊

🎨

Skill Swap Saturdays

Chef dad does a kids cooking class. Yoga mom teaches toddler stretches. Hidden talents, shared freely.

School Commute Buddy

Match with parents near the same school for carpools. Less traffic stress, more community on the daily commute.
